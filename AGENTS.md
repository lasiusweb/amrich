## Key Implementation Details

- **Sticky CTA bar** on product pages: fixed bottom on mobile (60px), inline right on desktop (50px). Appears at 25% scroll, opens slide-out quote form, transitions via `transform: translateY()`.
- **Quote form** collects: full name, phone, location (state/district), pond size (acres), target species, product interest, primary challenge, contact method (WhatsApp/Call/Email). Routes to regional sales via CRM/WhatsApp API.
- **Brochure download** in header, product pages, and footer.
- **Schema markup pattern**: `Product` + `Offer` + `HowTo` + `FAQPage` on product pages; `CollectionPage` + `ItemList` on categories; `Article` + `FAQPage` + `HowTo` on blog posts.
- **GA4 events**: `quote_form_open`, `quote_form_submit`, `brochure_download`, `scroll_depth`, `product_page_engagement`.
- **Consent mode**: India DPDP + GDPR compliant.
